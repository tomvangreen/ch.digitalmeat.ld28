Finally you have arrived.
You have waited a long time 
for this.

Your favorite act is playing
at the local venue.

After standing in line for
some time you got your ticket.


But you only got one...


Help your friends to get
to the dance floor.

Create distractions to
sneak your friends past
the guards.

And finally enjoy your
evening in the club.


Use the left and right
keys to walk around.

Press N to switch the
active person.

Press SPACE to interact
with an object.
